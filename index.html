My research interests include autonomous agents and multi-agent systems, distributed decision-making and agent coordination under uncertainty, human-AI collaboration, computational approaches in agent-driven systems, and scalable AI architectures with an emphasis on safety and trustworthiness.

This site keeps a small archive of projects, code experiments, films, and visual studies. Before working on AI tools and interfaces, I worked across film production, artistic practice, and technology entrepreneurship. Those experiences continue to shape how I think about attention, trust, and experience.

2025 · AI + Humans / HCI

Paper Plane: Anonymous Letters as Shared Urban Poetry

Paper Plane is an interactive application between a poetic game and an AI-mediated writing experience. The demo video documents the application rather than serving as the work itself. In the experience, a user writes an anonymous letter, folds it into a digital paper plane, and releases it into a city. Each flight produces an AI-generated ending, turning private writing into a shared urban poem.

Role: drawing, photography, writing

Stack / Tools: sketchbook, photography, image annotation

Art Works

2026 · AI Cinema / Film / Visual Research

Threshold

Threshold is a short experimental narrative about flooding, memory, and the fragile line between safety and disaster. Told through a first-person body-mounted perspective in contemporary Brooklyn, the film follows a person and their dog as a dream of cherry blossoms slowly gives way to rain, rising water, and a basement flood. The piece explores thresholds as both physical and emotional boundaries: a doorway, a warning line, a flood level, and the moment when care, attachment, and danger become inseparable.

Role: direction, editing, visual research

Stack / Tools: final cut pro, storyboards, kling 3.0
